Iskill Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Iskill ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that lets you browse, search, and save Claude AI Skills. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Favorites: Skills you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • Usage data: When you view or install a skill, we may increment an anonymous install counter stored in our database (Supabase). This counter is not linked to any personal identifier.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Iskill uses Supabase as its backend database to store and serve skill content. Only anonymous, aggregate data (such as install counts) is stored there. Please review Supabase's privacy policy for details on their data handling.

Children's Privacy

Iskill is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue at github.com/isink/iskill_ios .
